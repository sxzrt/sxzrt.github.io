About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Australian National University (ANU) under the supervision of Prof. Liang Zheng and Prof. Hongdong Li. I received my M.Sc from College of Computer and Control Engineering, Nankai University (NKU) in 2018, supervised by Prof. Jufeng Yang.

I am interested in exploring cutting-edge advancements in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Specifically, I am deeply intrigued by model and data vulnerability assessment, learning from synthetic data, and dataset representation strategies. Currently, my focus is on data-centric computer vision, involving comprehensive understanding of data vulnerability, developing robust evaluation protocols for computer vision tasks and uncovering underlying dataset distribution patterns.

I am lucky to have opportunities to work with Dr. Vivek Sharma (Sony AI), Dr. Nidham Gazagnadou (Sony AI), Dr. Lingjuan Lyu (Sony AI), Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng (Nankai University), Prof. Yu-Kun Lai and Prof. Paul Rosin (Cardiff University).
